5. Advertising

The Game may display ads, including rewarded ads that you can choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards. Our advertising partners may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve and measure ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

6. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored on servers managed by Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) located in the region. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Firebase Security Rules to restrict unauthorized access
  • Server-side receipt verification for in-app purchases
  • Duplicate login detection to protect your account

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Game progress data: Retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Purchase records: Retained as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Analytics data: Retained according to Firebase Analytics default retention periods.
  • Push notification tokens: Deleted when you disable notifications or uninstall the Game.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and game account.
  • Opt-out of notifications: Disable push notifications at any time through the in-game Settings menu or your device settings.
  • Opt-out of personalized ads: Adjust your device advertising settings as described in Section 5.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

9. Guest Accounts

If you use a guest account, your game data is tied to your device. If you change devices or uninstall the Game without linking a social account (Google or Apple), your data cannot be recovered. We strongly recommend linking a social account to preserve your progress.
